About Me

Educator turned
Cloud Engineer

I'm David Anuoluwapo Olofintila — a final-year student at Yaba College of Technology (in affiliation with the University of Nigeria, Nsukka), studying Computer & Robotics Education on a First-Class trajectory with a 4.57/5.00 GPA.

My journey has been shaped by a conviction that technology is most powerful when it is taught well. I've facilitated robotics workshops for secondary school students, led a 14-member executive board representing thousands of peers, and organised Nigeria's first campus Raspberry Pi club after attending the inaugural Lagos Pi Pico workshop.

"I'm now transitioning into cloud computing — having cleared AZ-900 and actively preparing for AZ-104 — because I believe infrastructure skills are the next frontier of my contribution."

Outside the classroom and the terminal, I advocate for SDG 4 (Quality Education) and SDG 5 (Gender Equality), and I hold a Grade 5 Distinction in Music Theory from ABRSM — proof that rigorous learning lives in many forms.
